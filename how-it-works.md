# How Guru For You Works

**Guru For You** is a real-time online classroom platform that connects teachers and students through live video, collaborative whiteboards, and AI-powered learning insights. Whether you're a teacher looking to grow your tutoring business, a student seeking personalized learning, or a parent wanting to track your child's progress — Guru For You has everything you need.

---

## For Teachers

### Get Started in Minutes

1. **Sign up** using Google or email — it's quick and free.
2. **Complete your profile** — add your subjects, qualifications, experience, class rate, and a short bio.
3. **Get approved** — our team reviews your profile to ensure quality for students.
4. **Start teaching** — once approved, you're ready to schedule and conduct live classes.

### Set Your Own Schedule

- Use the **weekly availability grid** to mark the days and times you're free to teach.
- Add **break times and holidays** so students only book when you're available.
- View your **projected earnings** based on your availability — see weekly and monthly estimates at a glance.

### Conduct Live Classes

- **HD Video & Audio** — teach face-to-face with your students using real-time video powered by WebRTC.
- **Screen Sharing** — share your screen to walk through presentations, documents, or coding exercises.
- **Interactive Whiteboard** — draw, annotate, and explain concepts visually in real time.
- **Live Chat** — exchange messages during the session for quick notes, links, or questions.
- **Session Timer** — track class duration in real time; the timer persists even if you refresh the page.

### AI-Vista Educator Suite

- **Zero Admin Workflow** — AI automatically generates executive-quality session reports after every class. No manual note-taking required.
- **Objective Growth Proof** — share AI-generated progress reports with parents to demonstrate measurable student improvement.
- **100% Revenue Ownership** — keep what you earn. No commission fees, no marketplace cuts. Just a flat platform fee of ₹30 per session.
- **Build Your Global Brand** — teach students from anywhere in the world and grow your independent tutoring practice.

### Points & Earnings

- **100 bonus points** awarded when you join the platform.
- **5 free classes** to get started — no points needed for your first five sessions.
- After free classes, a flat **₹30 platform fee** is deducted per session from your points balance.
- **Purchase point packs** anytime to keep teaching without interruption.
- **Track everything** — view your complete transaction history, total earnings, points balance, and class payment records from your dashboard.

## For Students

### Get Started Easily

1. **Sign up** using Google or email — choose the "Student" role.
2. **Complete your profile** — add your name, grade level, learning goals, and preferred subjects.
3. **Connect with teachers** — browse teacher profiles, check their expertise, and send a connection request.
4. **Start learning** — once a teacher accepts, you can join scheduled live classes.

### Join Live Classes

- **One-click classroom entry** — join your scheduled class directly from your dashboard.
- **HD Video & Audio** — see and hear your teacher clearly with real-time video.
- **Interactive Whiteboard** — follow along as your teacher draws and explains, or use the whiteboard yourself.
- **Live Chat** — ask questions, share notes, and interact during the session.
- **Screen Sharing** — view your teacher's screen for demonstrations and walkthroughs.

### Track Your Progress

- **Session History** — view all your past and upcoming classes with details like subject, date, duration, and teacher.
- **AI-Powered Reports** — receive detailed session summaries generated by AI after each class, covering key topics, engagement, and areas for improvement.
- **Progress Reports** — access periodic reports (monthly/quarterly) that track your learning trends and highlight strengths.

### Get Help Anytime

- **FAQ & Help Center** — find answers to common questions about getting started, classes, and platform features.
- **Support Tickets** — create a support ticket directly from your dashboard for any issue.
- **WhatsApp & Email Support** — reach out to our team instantly via WhatsApp or email.

## For Parents

### Stay in Control of Your Child's Education

Guru For You gives parents complete visibility and control over their child's learning journey — from enrollment to progress tracking.

### Enroll Your Child

- **Add your child** to the platform with their name, grade, subjects, and login credentials.
- **Manage passwords** — reset and update your child's account password anytime.
- **Profile management** — update your own profile and your child's information as needed.

### Manage Teacher Connections

- **Approve or reject** teacher connection requests for your child.
- **Pause or resume** access to any connected teacher at any time.
- **Full oversight** — you decide who teaches your child.

### Purchase Credits

- **Buy class credits** to book sessions for your child.
- **Flexible packages** available in both INR and USD:
  - INR: 10 classes (₹300), 20 classes (₹600), 50 classes (₹1,500)
  - USD: 5 classes ($4), 10 classes ($8), 15 classes ($11), 30 classes ($20)
- **Secure payments** powered by Razorpay.
- **Track your balance** — view credit balance and full transaction history from your dashboard.

### Monitor Sessions & Progress

- **Session Timeline** — view every attended session with subject, date, time, duration, teacher name, and status.
- **AI Session Reports** — receive detailed, AI-generated reports after each class covering:
  - Topics discussed and key takeaways
  - Student engagement and participation level
  - Strengths identified and areas for improvement
  - Actionable recommendations for next steps
- **Periodic Progress Reports** — access monthly and quarterly reports that track learning trends over time.
- **Assignment Tracking** — monitor your child's assignments, submission status, due dates, and scores.

### View Sample Reports

- Not sure what to expect? **View a sample AI session report** before purchasing credits — so you know exactly the kind of insights you'll receive.

## The Live Classroom Experience

Every Guru For You class happens in a purpose-built virtual classroom designed for interactive learning:

| Feature | Description |
|---|---|
| **HD Video Calling** | Crystal-clear, real-time video and audio powered by WebRTC peer-to-peer technology |
| **Screen Sharing** | Teachers can share their entire screen or specific windows for demonstrations |
| **Interactive Whiteboard** | Draw, write, and annotate in real time — both teachers and students can participate |
| **Live Chat** | Exchange messages, links, and notes instantly during the session |
| **Session Recording** | Classes are recorded with audio transcription for later review |
| **Persistent Timer** | Class duration is tracked accurately, even across page refreshes |
| **Works Everywhere** | Compatible with Chrome, Firefox, Edge, and Safari on desktop and mobile |

### Minimum Requirements

- **Internet**: 5 Mbps or higher (wired connection recommended)
- **Device**: Laptop, desktop, or tablet with a webcam and microphone
- **Browser**: Latest version of Chrome (recommended), Firefox, Edge, or Safari
- **Accessories**: Writing pad recommended for students, mandatory for teachers
